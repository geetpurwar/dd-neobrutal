FAQ

Good questions.

Is my audio private?+

Yes. Your audio is processed securely, used only to produce your transcription, and discarded immediately after. We never sell your data or use it to train third-party models.

How accurate is it — and does it remove filler words?+

Very. Doggy transcribes with near-perfect accuracy, then cleans up the result — removing filler words, fixing grammar, and formatting it.

Which apps does it work in?+

Any app that accepts keyboard input — Mail, Slack, VS Code, Notion, Word, your browser, and more. It works system-wide on your Mac.

How light is it, really?+

It idles around 0.1% CPU and roughly 100 MB of memory on our Macs — a menu-bar app, not a second operating system.

Which languages are supported?+

108 of them, including English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, and many more. The app automatically detects the language you're speaking.

What do I need to run it?+

A Mac running macOS 14.0 or later, plus an internet connection for the highest-accuracy AI models.

Does it work offline?+

Not yet — Doggy uses cloud AI models for top accuracy, so it needs an internet connection.

How does pricing work?+

You start with a free trial — 10,000 words, no card needed. After that, $5/mo for 20,000 words or $10/mo for 100,000 words. Cancel anytime.

Which microphones work?+

Any microphone macOS recognizes — the built-in mic, AirPods, or external USB and studio mics.
